Burglar Escape

My first completed game! A simple game written in Unity, which I've slowly been learning during the lock-down. Before tackling anything serious, I knew I had to see through something super simple. The result is "Burglar Escape". The goal is simple. You need to escape from the spotlight until the timer runs out.

Inspiration

In my Amiga days, I tried to self-learn Amos. This sparked a huge interest in finding game build in it, one of which was "The Great Escape of Billy Burglar". A super simple game yet kept me entertained for ages.
